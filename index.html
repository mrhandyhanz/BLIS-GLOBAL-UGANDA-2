Program Objectives

Becoming a Global

Instead of just learning to code, the program aims to open the learner's eyes to the world of technology and its impact on society. Empowering students to look at their communities scan and identify real-world struggles so they can stop being passive observers and start detect challenges that truly need solving

Unleashing Creative Innovation

The program encourages learners to trust their own ideas guiding them through the entire creative processes whether its planning,prototyping or manufacturing , the goal is to take a raw concept and nurture it until it transforms into a working piece ,helpful in society.

Breathing Life into Machines

This covers a hands-on approach for learners to build and control technology ,students will learn to design and develop robots,giving them a physical form and supplying them with energy and most importantly feeding the robot's brain with instructions(code) to bring them to life and perform useful tasks.

Building a Future Proof Career

The program bridges a gap between educationa and earning.It isn't just about making cool gadgets;it's about value,to equip learner's with enterprenuerial skills,teaching them how to take their software and robotic inventions and monetize them.This ensures that they leave the course not just with knowledge ,but with the ability to build a livelihood from their creativity.
