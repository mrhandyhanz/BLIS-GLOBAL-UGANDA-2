About ICRPlus

Bridging the Digital Divide in Uganda

What is ICRPlus?

ICRPlus is an Integrated Coding and Robotics Program designed to empower students with the technical, creative, and analytical skills required in the 21st-century global economy.

We believe that education should move beyond the textbook. Our program integrates hardware engineering (Robotics) with software logic (Coding) to create a holistic learning environment where students don't just consume technologyâ€”they build it.

Our Mission

To provide world-class STEAM education to every school in Uganda.

Our Vision

A future where Ugandan youth lead the global digital innovation space.

The Pillars of Our Program

Our methodology is built on four core foundations that ensure a balanced and future-ready educational experience.

Software Logic

Mastering languages like Scratch, Python, and C++ to control the digital world.

Hardware Eng.

Designing, building, and wiring physical robots and electronic systems.

Critical Thinking

Learning to break down complex problems into manageable, logical steps.

Creative Design

Using technology as a canvas for innovation and creative expression.
