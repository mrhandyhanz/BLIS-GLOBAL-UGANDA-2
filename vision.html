Our Vision for the 21st Century Learner

ICRPlus combines coding, robotics, and creative design to empower students and teachers with critical 21st-century skills. We aim to transform the learner into a catalyst for social-economic transformation.

Embedding Innovation within CBC

The main goal of this concept note by BLIS Global LTD is to imbed ICRPlus within the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC).

We equip learners with the abilities to scan and identify existing problems and challenges faced by world communities and industries.

"Act quickly on social-economic transformation."

Learner Competencies

At the end of this program, a learner shall be able to:

01

Detect the world’s community challenges.

02

Plan, conceptualize, design and develop appropriate solutions.

03

Turn developed solutions into software as well as Apps format (usable on mobile devices).

04

Design and develop human and industrial simulated Robots.

05

Supply robots with energy for intended movements.

06

Feed the robots brain with workable instructions for tasks.

07 — The Entrepreneurial Edge

Acquire entrepreneurial skills to monetize final and consumable Apps-based as well as robotics-based solutions.

The Physical Robotics Lab

The Program includes the installation of a Physical Robotics Lab at your School; which will serve as both a training facility and a resource for ongoing and future-facing learning.
